About Me

My name is Benjamin Jones. I am a dedicated and hardworking individual currently pursuing a degree in accounting at Brigham Young University, Provo, UT. I have a strong academic background with a 3.97 GPA and a high school diploma from Hamilton High School, Chandler, AZ, where I graduated with a weighted GPA of 4.2.

I am bilingual, fluent in both English and Spanish, which I developed during my 2-year volunteer missionary service in North Argentina. This experience not only helped me master a new language but also instilled in me a strong work ethic and the ability to stay focused and committed.

Professionally, I have held various roles that have honed my skills in customer service, management, and logistics. I am currently the manager of a summer sales pest control team at Blue Penguin Pest Control. Previously, I worked as a delivery truck driver and warehouse employee at RWC International Phoenix AZ, provided outside services and customer service at Ocotillo Golf Course, and worked as a cook and customer service employee at Five Guys.

In addition to my professional experience, I have been actively involved in volunteer work. I have volunteered with Feed My Starving Children in Phoenix, AZ, where I performed various maintenance tasks and coordinated efforts with other volunteers. I also participated in fundraising activities for Huskies Helping Huskies, a program aimed at supporting underprivileged students.

I am passionate about problem-solving, working with people, and handling finances. I am always eager to take on new challenges and am flexible in performing various functions. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me!
